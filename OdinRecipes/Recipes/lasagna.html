Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is rich, cheesy, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1kg ground beef
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar meat sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet.
    3. Layer noodles, sauce, and beef in a baking dish.
    4. Top with mozzarella cheese.
    5. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
    6. Let cool for 10 minutes, then serve and enjoy!
