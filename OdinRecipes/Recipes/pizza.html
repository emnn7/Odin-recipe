Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe includes a deliciously crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and a layer of gooey cheese and toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and spread the tomato sauce evenly over it.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  4. Add your preferred toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil, slice, and serve hot.
